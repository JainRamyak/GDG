Recipe 1.-

Chana Masala

Ingredients

1 cup dried chickpeas (or 1 can chickpeas) 2 tbsp oil 1 large onion (chopped) 2 tomatoes (chopped) 1 tsp cumin seeds 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste 1 tsp ground turmeric, cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala 1 tsp red chili powder 1 tsp amchur (or lemon juice) Salt to taste Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook Chickpeas (if using dried): Soak overnight, boil, and drain. Skip this step if using canned.
  2. Sauté: Heat oil, add cumin seeds, then onions. Cook until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Add Tomatoes & Spices: Add tomatoes, cook until soft. Add all spices and salt.
  4. Combine: Add chickpeas and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Finish: Stir in garam masala, amchur/lemon juice. Garnish with coriander.

Recipe 2.-

Aloo Gobi

Ingredients

2 medium potatoes (cubed) 1 small cauliflower (cut into florets) 2 tbsp oil 1 large onion (chopped) 2 tomatoes (chopped) 1 tsp cumin seeds 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste 1 tsp turmeric, cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala 1 tsp red chili powder Salt to taste Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Sauté Potatoes & Cauliflower: Heat oil, add cumin seeds, then potatoes and cauliflower. Cook until lightly golden.
  2. Cook Onion & Spices: Add onions, sauté until golden. Stir in ginger-garlic paste, then add tomatoes and spices. Cook until soft.
  3. Combine & Simmer: Mix in the potatoes and cauliflower. Cover and cook for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Finish: Stir in garam masala, adjust seasoning, and garnish with fresh coriander.

Recipe 3.-

Palak Paneer

Ingredients

200g paneer (cubed) 4 cups spinach (blanched & pureed) 2 tbsp oil or ghee 1 onion (chopped) 2 tomatoes (chopped) 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste 1 tsp cumin seeds 1 tsp cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric, garam masala Salt to taste Fresh cream (optional)

Steps

  1. Sauté Paneer: Lightly fry paneer cubes until golden, set aside.
  2. Cook Spices: Heat oil, add cumin seeds, then onions. Sauté until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and spices. Cook until soft.
  3. Add Spinach: Stir in spinach puree, simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Combine: Add paneer and garam masala. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Finish: Optional – add a drizzle of cream and serve with naan or rice.

Recipe 4.-

Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients

1 cup basmati rice (soaked) 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans, potato) 2 tbsp oil or ghee 1 onion (sliced) 2 tomatoes (chopped) 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste 1 tsp cumin seeds 1 bay leaf, 2-3 cloves, 2-3 cardamom pods, 1 cinnamon stick 1 tsp turmeric, cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala Salt to taste Fresh coriander & mint for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook Rice: Boil rice until 70% done, drain, and set aside.
  2. Sauté Vegetables: Heat oil, add whole spices, then onions. Cook until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and powdered spices. Cook for a few minutes.
  3. Add Vegetables: Add mixed veggies and cook until tender.
  4. Layer Biryani: In a pot, layer half the rice, add the vegetable mixture, then top with remaining rice. Garnish with coriander and mint.
  5. Steam: Cover and cook on low heat (dum) for 10-15 minutes. Serve with raita.

Recipe 5.-

Gajar Halwa

Ingredients

4 cups grated carrots 2 cups milk 1 cup sugar 3 tbsp ghee ½ cup condensed milk (optional) ¼ cup chopped nuts (almonds, cashews) 1 tsp cardamom powder

Steps

  1. Cook Carrots: Heat ghee in a pan, add grated carrots. Sauté for 5-7 minutes until soft.
  2. Add Milk: Pour in milk and cook until carrots absorb the milk (about 15-20 minutes), stirring frequently.
  3. Sweeten: Add sugar and condensed milk (if using), cook until the halwa thickens and ghee starts separating.
  4. Finish: Stir in cardamom powder and nuts. Serve warm, garnished with more nuts.
